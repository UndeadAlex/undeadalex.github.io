Breakout

The classic breakout made using SFML.

Project Date: January 2024

Description

While working on the John Horton projects, they reminded me of some older, classic games. One in particular that really inspired a younger me was a game called “Breakout,” or “Brick Breaker” as I knew it. A simple, iconic, and fun experience, it seemed perfect for my next SFML project.

The goal wasn’t to create a perfect facsimile of Breakout, but instead to attempt to make a version of it just from looking at gameplay to test my skills and hopefully learn some new ones, I would say I achieved a rather good creation that is good for a bit of fun or for a small break.
